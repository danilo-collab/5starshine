# 5 Star Shine Auto Detailing

## Product Purpose
Landing page for a premium mobile auto detailing and ceramic coating business in Phoenix, AZ. The site converts visitors into bookings via phone calls and contact form submissions.

## Users
- Vehicle owners in the Phoenix/Scottsdale/Mesa metro area
- Luxury and enthusiast car owners seeking premium detailing
- People searching for mobile detailing convenience
- Clients interested in long-term ceramic coating protection

## Brand Voice
Confident, premium, trustworthy. Not luxury-pretentious — approachable expertise. The business is owner-operated, hands-on, results-driven.

## Anti-references
- Generic white SaaS landing pages
- Overly corporate auto dealership sites
- Cheap coupon/discount-driven detailing sites
- Template-looking dark themes with random gradients

## Strategic Principles
- Results-first: show the work, let quality speak
- Convenience messaging: mobile service is the differentiator
- Trust signals: certifications, insurance, reviews
- Clear CTAs: phone number and booking always visible
